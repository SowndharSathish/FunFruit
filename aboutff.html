About Us

Fun Fruit was born with the name Tepehuana, which refers to the Sierra de Tepehuanes, located south of Chihuahua and part of Durango, where the brand originates.

In 2000, Fun Fruit was founded with an image of a happy Sun above the mountain that refers to the nature of our products and how friendly they are for health.
